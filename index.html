About Me

I'm known as Uno, but you might recognize me online as yqroo. My passion lies in the world of cybersecurity, and I'm an avid participant in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. My goal is to carve out a path in the realm of cyber defense and become a skilled security engineer. Currently, I'm an undergraduate student in Computer Science at IPB University.

Apart from my hands-on engagement in CTF challenges, I also enjoy sharing my knowledge with others. I regularly write detailed write-ups for various CTF challenges, providing insights into the solutions and thought processes behind them. You can read my write-ups here, and by doing so, I hope to contribute to the collective learning within the cybersecurity community. With a fervent curiosity for all things related to online security, I'm on a journey to strengthen digital defenses and make the digital landscape a safer place for everyone.
